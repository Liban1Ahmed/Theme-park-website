Accessibility Information

At our theme park, we embrace all visitors and aim to provide an unforgettable experience for everyone. We have fully equipped our park to cater to the needs of all our guests, including those who require additional accessibility features.

Our park offers wheelchair rentals and has conveniently located accessible restrooms. For guests with hearing impairments, we offer sign language interpretation services for our live shows. Visual assistance is also available upon request for visitors with visual impairments.

Our highly trained staff are always ready to assist with any specific needs and to ensure all our rides, shows, dining and shopping facilities are accessible to everyone.

To learn more about our accessible services or to discuss any specific needs for your visit, please contact our customer services team. We are committed to offering a fun and safe environment where everyone can enjoy their time.
